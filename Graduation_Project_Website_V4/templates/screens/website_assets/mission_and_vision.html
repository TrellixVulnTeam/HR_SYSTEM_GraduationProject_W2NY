MISSION

Our mission is helping applicants to find jobs faster and easily. Also, to help companies to find qualified employees. The main target of the project is to support the social distance by making an online interview to filter the applicants and when some applicants accepted they can make the actual interview by online meeting.

VISION

Our vision is to support the online meeting after getting accepted without any physical interaction. Our goal is to make the website is global and make us as a brand to support all companies around the world and make the website is fully automated instead of HR employees by bot.
